Differences between inline tags

Hint, to reach the answer you have to click on the summary (ex: [b, strong])

[b, strong]
  • b is used for the purpose of styling but has no meaning to the SEO
  • on the other hand strong tag is used for styling but also represent a special meaning to the word in it and have to do with SEO
[i, em]
  • i is used for the purpose of styling but has no meaning to the SEO
  • on the other hand em tag is used for styling but also represent a special meaning to the word in it and have to do with SEO as it's used to put more stress on a word as It has a semantic importance and improving accessibility.
[s, [del, ins]]
  • s (strikethrough), or a line through it. used to represent things that are no longer relevant or no longer accurate.
  • However, s is not appropriate when indicating document; for that we should use the del and ins elemnts.
